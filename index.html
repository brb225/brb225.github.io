Benjamin R. Baer

Bio

Hi, my name is Ben. I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of St Andrews in Scotland. Previously, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Rochester. I completed my PhD at Cornell University under the supervision of Marty Wells.

My research interests include addressing various forms of missing data such as causal inference and survival analysis, efficient estimation, and general statistical methodology.

B.S. in Mathematics | September 2011 - May 2015

Experience

Amazon | Applied Scientist | May 2019 - August 2019
