Benjamin R. Baer

Bio

Ben Baer studies causal inference and survival analysis at the University of Rochester, under the mentorship of Ashkan Ertefaie and Rob Strawderman. He completed his PhD under the supervision of Marty Wells in interpretable hypothesis testing and exponential family matrix modelling. He is slowly developing regression penalties to encourage structured sparsity.

He enjoys gardening, bird watching, and finding his cats newest hiding spots.
