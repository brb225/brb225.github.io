Benjamin R. Baer

Bio

Hi, my name is Ben. I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Bowdoin College in Maine and an Honorary Lecturer in Statistics at the University of St Andrews.

My research interests are broadly in applied/methodological statistics, with a particular focus on missing data problems such as causal inference, survival analysis, and ecological survey methods, as well as efficient estimation. I especially enjoy developing statistical methods that are motivated by scientific questions and tailored to real applications.
